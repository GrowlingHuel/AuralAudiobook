Aural Audiobook Adventures: The Manifesto
I. The Core Vision To create the world’s most intimate and customizable reading experience by merging neural text-to-speech with a spatial, interactive interface. The app is not just a player; it is an Acoustic Alchemy Lab where the narrator is as much a choice as the book.

II. The Pillars of Excellence

Acoustic Alchemy: Every voice must be high-fidelity (24kHz Mono). Blending must be smooth (KNN-5) and intuitive, allowing for infinite "hybrid" personalities.

The Boundless Library: Total accessibility for both the Great Classics (Gutenberg) and the Private Manuscript (The Vault).

Gapless Immersion: The transition between sentences must be invisible. The engine must always be "one step ahead" of the listener’s ear.

Visual Literacy: The interface must always show the user what is being read. The text and the voice are one unified performance.

III. The Technical Benchmark (The Standard for Refinement)

Audio Purity: Zero digital clipping, zero buffer pops, and zero "robotic" artifacts. Audio must maintain a soft-limited headroom.

Responsiveness: The Nebula UI must maintain 60FPS on high-end hardware while the background synthesis (Inference) runs on isolated threads.

Persistence: The "Vault" must be a stable, localized environment where user data is never lost.

Diagnostic Integrity: Every error must be measurable. We do not "guess" at distortion; we analyze peak data and buffer logs.

IV. Success Definition Success is achieved when a user can import their own written work, select a custom-blended "Viking/Professor" voice, and listen to a 30-minute chapter with the same seamless quality as a professional studio recording.
